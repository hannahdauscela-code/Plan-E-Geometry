Area and Perimeter

Square

Area = s²

Perimeter = 4s

Rectangle

Area = length × width

Perimeter = 2(length + width)

Triangle

Area = ½ × base × height

Perimeter = sum of all sides

Circle

Area = πr²

Circumference = 2πr

Properties of Shapes

Square

  • 4 equal sides
  • 4 right angles
  • Diagonals are equal

Rectangle

  • Opposite sides equal
  • 4 right angles

Triangle

  • Interior angles sum to 180°

Circle

  • All points are equidistant from the center

Triangle Congruence

SSS (Side–Side–Side)

All three sides are equal

SAS (Side–Angle–Side)

Two sides and the included angle are equal

ASA (Angle–Side–Angle)

Two angles and the included side are equal

AAS (Angle–Angle–Side)

Two angles and a non-included side are equal

HL (Hypotenuse–Leg)

Hypotenuse and one leg are equal (right triangles only)

Similarity of Figures

AA Similarity

Two angles are equal

SAS Similarity

Two sides are proportional and the included angle is equal

SSS Similarity

All three sides are proportional

Corresponding Sides

Corresponding sides are proportional

Coordinate Geometry

Distance Formula

√[(x₂ − x₁)² + (y₂ − y₁)²]

Midpoint Formula

((x₁ + x₂)/2 , (y₁ + y₂)/2)

Slope Formula

(y₂ − y₁) / (x₂ − x₁)
